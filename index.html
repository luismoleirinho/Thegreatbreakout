40.000 KMS FROM EUROPE'S WESTERNMOST POINT TO THE END OF THE ROAD IN SIBERIA AND BACK

Breaking the routine has never been so important! Still facing the repercussions of a pandemic that put everything and everyone on standby, we all feel a great desire to go chase the freedom we once knew and reconnect with others. The Great Breakout is exactly that, my need of stepping out from a stagnant reality and restore a bit of faith in humanity. The goal is to connect two extreme points: from Cabo da Roca, Portugal to Magadan, Siberia. About 40.000 kms in 6 months crossing over 20 countries and going through the most diverse landscapes and cultures. The world changes very fast day by day and in the end we know nothing about it, is it gonna be possible? There's only one way to find out!

WHO'S THE GENIOUS BEHIND ALL THIS?

Luís Moleirinho is a true motorcycle enthusiast! Born in 1992 in Lisbon, Portugal. He spent most of his life skateboarding and involved in the Portuguese hardcore punk scene. In 2012 while doing volunteer work in eastern europe he started hitchhiking all over the continent, these experiences made him discover a great passion for travelling and for understanding the world we live in and the people we live with. Later on he bought his first motorcycle and from a simple way of commuting, it quickly became a means of exploring whatever was around him. And just like that a great love for adventure motorcycling was born! The legend says that Luis never touched a 4 wheel vehicle again. He studied cinema and photography and now he's currently in need of gas money, so feel free to scroll down.
